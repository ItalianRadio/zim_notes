====== Eidolons ======
Created Tuesday 28 May 2019 - [[ordum:People|back]]

Beings born of Doar'tz in their image. You may not play an eidolon as eidolons have no real thoughts of their own, they follow the will of their creator, never thinking, merely reacting.
